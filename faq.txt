Q: What services does Sunlite provide?
A: Sunlite provides reliable solar power system installation, long-term maintenance, and flexible financing options for Nigerian homes and small businesses.

Q: How long does a typical solar system last?
A: A typical high-quality solar power system installed by Sunlite has a lifespan of 25 to 30 years with proper, regular maintenance.

Q: Can I use solar power during cloudy weather or at night?
A: Yes. Your system includes a battery storage unit (inverter) that stores the energy captured during the day. This stored energy is what you use when it is cloudy or after sunset.

Q: Yaya ake hada solar? (How is solar installed?)
A: Ana fara binciken wurin da za a yi aiki, sannan a sanya panels (fankokin hasken rana) a saman gini, sannan a haɗa su da inverter da baturi don ajiye wutar lantarki. Ana yin duk wannan ta ƙwararrun injiniyoyin Sunlite.

Q: Menene amfanin solar? (What are the benefits of solar?)
A: Babban amfanin solar shine rage dogaro ga wutar lantarki ta grid, rage kudin wutar lantarki, da kuma samar da wutar lantarki mai tsabta wacce ba ta gurbata muhalli.
