Q: Yaya ake yin amfani da solar? (How do I use solar?)
A: Ana amfani da hasken rana ta hanyar shigar da panels a saman gini don samar da wutar lantarki. Wannan yana rage dogaro da grid kuma yana rage kudin wutar lantarki. (Solar power is used by installing panels on a roof to generate electricity. This reduces reliance on the grid and lowers electricity costs.)
